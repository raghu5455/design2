About Us

About Jill

Welcome to your bridge between the skilled trades and your career aspirations. I am Jill, a seasoned Human Resources professional with over two decades of experience, the majority of which I've dedicated to the manufacturing and technology industries. My journey has evolved from overseeing HR departments to becoming a Certified Career Coach, specializing in the nuanced world of skilled trades careers.

My Mission

I've noticed a significant gap in career coaching services – a lack of specialized support for the skilled trades. This realization fueled my mission to create a tailored suite of services that not only includes traditional resume writing but also extends into the essential aspects of career navigation within skilled trades. My aim is to empower you with the knowledge and tools to confidently negotiate your wage, understand your benefits package, and decode your pay statements.

Why Choose Me?

My background as a Human Resources Director, primarily in sectors that heavily employ skilled trades, provides me with a unique lens through which I view career development. Combined with my certification as a Career Coach, I offer a blend of practical HR expertise and personalized coaching. This dual perspective is rare in the career coaching landscape, particularly for those in skilled trades seeking to navigate their careers effectively.

I understand the challenges and opportunities within these industries deeply. Whether you're just starting out, looking to advance in your current role, or seeking a complete career pivot, I'm here to guide you through every step with personalized advice and strategies tailored to the skilled trades.

In a world where skilled trades are often overlooked by traditional career coaching services, I stand as a beacon for those seeking specialized support. Let's embark on this journey together, unlocking the doors to your potential and paving the way for a fulfilling and prosperous career.
