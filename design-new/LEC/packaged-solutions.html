Secure Private Cellular Network

KEEP VITAL UTILITIES OPERATIONAL DURING UNPLANNED EVENTS

Our secure private LTE network can provide the same dedicated broadband network used by first responders. With no throttling anywhere in the country and geographical redundancy. Ensure your vital utilities remain online even during a crisis.

Includes:

  • Cybersecurity: your network is secured proactively with a layered approach
  • Verizon, AT&T, US Cellular and FirstNet (built by AT&T) are our network options
  • Carrier Agnostic-Connectivity Centric Approach
  • Cross carrier connectivity – you are not locked in to using a single carrier
  • Single point of contact for support, no carrier interaction required
  • Geographical redundance
  • All connections are logged and monitored, with attacks and malicious traffic swiftly thwarted in real-time, around the clock, 365 days a year.
