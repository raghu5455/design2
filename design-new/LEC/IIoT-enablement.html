What is IIoT?

The latest IoT Analytics “State of IoT—Spring 2023” report shows that the number of global IoT connections grew by 18% in 2022 to 14.3 billion active IoT endpoints. In 2023, IoT Analytics expects the global number of connected IoT devices to grow another 16%, to 16.7 billion active endpoints. According to their analysis, by 2027, there will likely be more than 29 billion IoT connections.

The New Era of Industry

IIoT enables companies to pick up on inefficiencies and problems sooner, saving time and money and supporting business intelligence efforts. In the industrial vertical, IIoT holds great potential for quality control, sustainable and green practices, supply chain traceability and overall supply chain efficiency.
IIoT technology, coupled with advances in data collection and analytics, gives business managers at all levels more relevant information than ever before.
