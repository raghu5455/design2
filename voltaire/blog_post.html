Voltaire Cannabis Receives Final License, Grand Opening Coming Soon

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Voltaire Cannabis Receives Its Final License, Plans October 2023 Grand Opening of First Location in Mount Holly, New Jersey

Mount Holly, NJ (August 18, 2023) – Voltaire Cannabis is thrilled to announce that the company has received its final license from the New Jersey Cannabis Regulatory Commission (CRC) to open its first retail location in Mount Holly, NJ. This marks a major milestone for Voltaire, allowing the company to move forward with its mission of delivering unmatched service with unmatched products at unmatched prices.

Staying true to the company’s refined aesthetic and elevated experience, the store is slated to open in October of 2023 and will feature a luxurious retail environment outfitted with upscale interior finishes, knowledgeable staff, and a carefully curated menu of premium cannabis flower, extracts, edibles, and accessories.

“We look forward to bringing the philosophy of Voltaire to life. A dispensary centered upon enjoying life while doing good,” said Leo Ashe, CEO of Voltaire Cannabis. “After years of preparation and hard work, we are thrilled to have received the green light from the CRC. We look forward to enriching the Mount Holly community and setting the standard for exceptional service, ethics, and product selection in the New Jersey cannabis industry.”

Voltaire Cannabis stands apart through its meticulous focus on customer experience, personalized recommendations from highly-trained Cannasseurs, a large and elegant environment, carefully vetted product partners, and a clear mission. As part of Voltaire’s commitment to social responsibility, the company plans to donate a minimum of 2% of their profit towards the community, social justice, and sustainability.

Voltaire invites customers to visit VoltaireCannabis.com for an invitation to their Grand Opening and stay up to date with their latest news and offerings. Those interested in working in the cannabis industry are also encouraged to apply for one of their open positions via VoltaireCannabis.com/careers.

About Voltaire Cannabis: Voltaire Cannabis is a progress-driven company dedicated to revolutionizing the cannabis retail landscape. With a steadfast commitment to quality, education, and community, Voltaire Cannabis aims to provide customers with a premium dispensary experience that exceeds expectations. By partnering with local organizations and adhering to the highest industry standards, the company seeks to contribute to the growth and well-being of the communities it serves.
