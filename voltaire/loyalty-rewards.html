HOW THE VOLTAIRE REWARDS PROGRAM WORKS:

  1. Sign up at a Voltaire Store.
  2. Earn 1 point for every $1 spent AND receive 5% off regularly priced items.
  3. Access your wallet and check your points any time.
  4. Redeem your points to use for in-store purchases.

VOLTAIRE POINT REDEMPTIONS:

  • 150 points = $5 off

  • 300 points = $10 off

  • 500 points = $20 off

  • 1000 points = $50 off

Important Disclaimers:

  • The final order total must exceed $0.01 when redeeming a rewards offer.

  • Certain restrictions may apply when redeeming points in conjunction with a promotion or discount.

  • The 5% discount does not apply to gift cards or items already on sale.

  • Discounts cannot be stacked with other discounts or promos.

  • Products and promos are available while supplies last.

  • This rewards program and any related terms or discounts) may change at any time. Please check this page to see if any changes have been made.
