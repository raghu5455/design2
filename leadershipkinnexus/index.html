LK Publishing Vision Statement

Learn more

LK Publications – from Leadership Kinnexus – is a company that focuses on leadership and community texts, arguments, philosophies, and best practices. Compared to other leadership publishing organizations, LK focuses on how the leader and the led interact.

The dynamic relationship between these two components of leadership is what makes it hard to understand, and certainly to create and replicate. But not impossible. Join us in reading and understanding – and arguing – about what good leadership looks like, and how we can join in this dynamic interaction.

Art And The Aesthetics Of The Everyday

By Betty A. Block
Award-winning Author

In this book Betty Block invites us to consider the aesthetic and philosophic framework that underlies Kleinlein’s work. In a wide-ranging analysis, Block examines Kleinlein’s paintings through the lens of philosophers such as Plato, Merleau-Ponty, Husserl, Heidegger, and current thought on the aesthetics of the everyday. Her personal interviews with Kleinlein provide an intimate look into the life and work of this artist and the way he views our world.

Leadership Kinnexus is a company that focuses on titles that address leadership and community.

These ideas are two sides of the same coin, and we hope to make this company a business where teams and leaders can put their ideas into digital and hard copy print.

The idea of “kinnexus” began as a joke – we wanted to use this word years ago as a pun for the words “connect us,” but we were talked out of it by our late, good friend Bill Forbus. “Too cute” was his sentiment – the idea of connecting colleagues in kinesiology didn’t work for him.
