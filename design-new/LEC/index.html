iQ2 Web Based Platform

iQ2™ is our award-winning, web-based IIoT Platform. Designed for flexibility, security, and integration simplicity with existing operational assets, iQ2 has a become a highly respected solution for reliable remote monitor and control. Water/Wastewater, Oil and Gas, Utility, and more count on iQ2 every day to provide real-time alarming, analytics, reporting, and compliance to mission critical field assets and operational systems. Cost effectiveness of iQ2 helps drive savings of money, time, and resources.
