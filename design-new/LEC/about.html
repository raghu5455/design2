About us

As an industry or municipality, you must be proactive in your ability to service your customer, become more profitable, reduced waste and risk and ensure compliance. Simply put, LEC develops and provides the tools that your team needs to accomplish these goals.


30 years of Industrial Automation and Controls and Engineering experience continues to drive success for our customers and the development of industry-leading web-based solution technologies. Our passion for the needs of our customers is evident from the ROI generated with our products and services.

Commitment to Excellence

We uphold the quality of our services and actively solicit client feedback, assessing the performance data of both our services and products. We gauge not only the outcomes but also the professionalism of our dedicated staff.

Measurable Impact

We meticulously track and assess the impact of our products, services, expertise, and professionalism. This ensures that we consistently deliver the exceptional level of service, value for money, and end-results that our clients anticipate.

Pinnacle of Performance

LEC epitomizes service excellence, gauged through client feedback. Our operational excellence is mirrored in client processes and financial metrics. Employee excellence is showcased by our team's expertise and drive to propel sustainable performance forward.

Our Team

“As LEC continues to increase in staff size and Client base, our ultimate goal is to expand our knowledge and skill set in order to grow BETTER, not just bigger.”

We've seen many changes in the 30 years since we opened our doors. When we started out in 1989, we were originally focused solely on industrial and manufacturing projects. Since then we have grown into an internationally recognized leader in providing quality integrated systems and experienced IIoT enablement for Clients all over the world.

Our Mission

At LEC, we deliver top-tier industrial automation and control engineering, along with industrial IoT enablement and web platform services. Our unwavering commitment extends to our clients, strategic partners, and the communities we serve, ensuring the highest quality at every step.

Our Vision

As a company, we are dedicated to helping our clients achieve and maintain the highest levels of production and performance. In doing so, each member of the LEC Team is committed to delivering superior services. We regularly evaluate our own actions and seek feedback from our clients to help us expand our services and products.
