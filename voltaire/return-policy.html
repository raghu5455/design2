RETURN / EXCHANGE POLICY

At Voltaire, you can buy with confidence, knowing that we are dedicated to providing you with the highest quality products and service. In accordance with the laws and regulations of the Cannabis Regulatory Commission (CRC) and the State of New Jersey, all of our cannabis products are:
 

  1. Rigorously tested by a CRC-licensed third-party to ensure safety and quality;

  2. Meticulously packaged; and

  3. Carefully inspected both before and after arriving at our store.

We strive to satisfy every customer, but if there's a problem with your purchase, please bring it back and we will try to make it right!

LEO ASHE | CEO

Return Policy For Cannabis Products:

  • Voltaire will exchange damaged or defective cannabis products or cannabis-related accessories (e.g., bongs, rolling papers, etc.) for up to seven (7) days after purchase. To be eligible for exchange, the original receipt must accompany any damaged or defective products. Refunds are not permitted. All exchanges shall be at the discretion of Voltaire. Non-damaged or defective product sales are final.

Return Policy For Non-Cannabis Merchandise:

  • Voltaire will exchange unworn/non-used merchandise for up to fourteen (14) days after purchase. To be eligible for exchange, the merchandise must include the original receipt and be in suitable condition for resale, with original tags attached. Refunds are not permitted. All exchanges shall be at the discretion of Voltaire.

For Further Information:

  • If you have any questions about our return / exchange policy or need further assistance, please email us (returns [at] shopvoltaire.com) or visit one of our stores and ask to speak with a manager.
