  • Voltaire will proactively connect with and listen to neighboring residents / businesses, while investing extensive time and funding towards helping revitalize local neighborhoods.

[GENUINE] SUSTAINABILITY

  • Voltaire's "Sustainability Task Force" will consider the environment within every aspect of our operations and regularly search for new ways to lower our footprint while increasing sustainability within the cannabis industry.

[GENUINE] ACTIVISM

  • Voltaire's "Racial Justice Task Force" will continuously look for new ways to encourage diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) internally and throughout the cannabis industry.
