Gathering Spot

In front of Barefoot Beach Cafe on the ocean side (look for the cafe umbrella)


Barefoot Beach Cafe

With the Waikiki hotels behind you, walk along the beach towards Kapiolani Park. Just past the white lifeguard tower on the beach, the "2F" lifeguard tower, you will find Barefoot Beach Cafe, popular among locals. Please come to the front of the cafe on the ocean side. After gathering, we will move to the shade of the trees in the park, so please come early so you don't get late. It's about a 15-minute walk from the Moana Surfrider. Enjoy a morning stroll through the beach park.
